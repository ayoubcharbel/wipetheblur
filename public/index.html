Bot Features

Real-time Tracking

Automatically tracks every message and sticker sent by users in your Telegram group. Get instant updates on group activity and engagement levels.

Dynamic Leaderboards

View comprehensive leaderboards showing top users by messages, stickers, and total activity. Encourage friendly competition in your group.

Personal Statistics

Each user can view their personal stats including total messages sent, stickers used, and their ranking within the group.

Persistent Data

All user statistics are saved permanently. Scores accumulate over time without daily resets, building a complete activity history.

Live Dashboard

Access a web-based admin panel to monitor bot status, view detailed statistics, and track group activity trends in real-time.

24/7 Uptime

Hosted on reliable cloud infrastructure ensuring your bot is always online and tracking activity around the clock.

Bot Commands

/start

Initialize the bot and display welcome message with available commands.

/leaderboard

View the current leaderboard showing top users by messages, stickers, and total score.

/mystats

Display your personal statistics including messages sent, stickers used, and ranking.

/help

Show help information and list of all available bot commands.

How Scoring Works

1 point per message sent
1 point per sticker sent

Scores accumulate over time and never reset!
